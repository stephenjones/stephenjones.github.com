<p><b>Closed - Life Drawing in Melbourne/St. Kilda (Linden Gallery)</b></p>

   * Update (December-2012): Unfortunately we have decided to close this Life Drawing session down and it will no longer run :( - for details of other Life Drawing sessions that you might be interested contact Phil Kreveld on 0418 530 781

<p>For those looking for a friendly group to draw and paint with, this is it. I have been going along to this group for some years now - there is no booking, just turn up to try it out, or come along on a social basis.</p> 

   * Tuesday nights, 6-9pm, $20
   * Single long pose, usually the same model over two weeks - suitable for drawing and painting
